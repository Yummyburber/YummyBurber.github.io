Overview

The Dubuque Coop was founded in 2012 by several biking and community advocates that saw a need for a bicycle cooperative in Dubuque to help people get on bikes. Our shop is located 2206 Central Ave in Dubuque.

We are a registered 501c3 operating as Dubuque Bikes, Inc. dba the Dubuque Bike Coop.

We operate under the guidance of a board of directors, which for the 2025-2026 fiscal year is as follows:

    • Mission:

    The Dubuque Bike Coop’s mission is to help build a community that promotes, uses and values bicycling as a viable and sustainable means of transportation, health and overall wellness. We do this by:

  • Providing access to reliable, low-cost bicycles
  • Providing educational opportunities on bicycle maintenance and repair
  • Promoting a mutual respect between bicyclists, drivers and other pedestrians
  • Advocating for improved and/or new bicycling infrastructure
  • Practicing and encouraging environment sustainability
