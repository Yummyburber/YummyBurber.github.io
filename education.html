Education is a large part of creating a strong and self-reliant bicycling community.

For bicycle maintenance and repair education, we offer periodic clinics at our shop or at a other sites such as the public library. These clinics can range from simple bicycle checks to tube/tire changes to full bicycle overhauls. Please reach out or follow us on Facebook for more information.

In support of our education mission, we also teach riders of all ages how to ride safely, confidently and respectfully.
