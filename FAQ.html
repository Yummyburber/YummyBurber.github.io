  • What is the Dubuque Bike Coop?
    • We're glad that you asked! We are a non-profit, volunteer-driven community bike project. Founded in 2012, our goal is to help foster the growing bicycling community in the Dubuque area.
  • Hey – I’ve got this amazing bike/component/accessory that I don’t use anymore… will you buy it from me?
    • No. We only accept donated items.
  • Hey – will you fix my bike for me?
    • No. Let us explain: we are a community bike project – not a bike shop. We will be more than happy to teach you how to maintain or repair your bike (see RAB), but we cannot do the work completely for you. If you’re looking for someone to do the work for you, we recommend that you support one of the several great local bike shops (LBS) in the area.
  • What is Rent-A-Bench (RAB)?
    • We’re not going to lie… we have a pretty sweet setup of tools. Even better is that we want to share them with YOU! For $10/hour, you get access to a repair stand, tools and the tribal knowledge of anyone in the shop. In addition to the standard fee, we’re also big fans of pizza, cookies, brownies and cupcakes. Sorry, we do not loan out tools.
  • I love what you all are doing for the community… Is there anything I can do to help you all out?
    • Absolutely! First and foremost, you can RIDE YOUR BIKE! This helps us realize our overall goal – to get more people on bikes. Secondly, you can help SPREAD THE WORD about our work and our mission. We are here to help and the more connections we have within the community, the more we can do just that! Las but not lease, you can VOLUNTEER! We are always looking for volunteers to help the organization run smoothly. Opportunities include mechanics, website/social media, community outreach,and bookkeeping/administration. Please contact us for more details.
